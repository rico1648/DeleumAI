6. Training and Staying Informed

We're committed to helping you use AI tools effectively and safely.

  • **Mandatory Training:** All employees who use AI tools *must* receive training on this policy and related best practices.
  • **ICT Department Support:** The ICT Department is responsible for providing the necessary training and support. Don't hesitate to reach out to them if you have questions or need assistance.
