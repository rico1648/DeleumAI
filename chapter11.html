10. Using AI Tools for Personal Purposes

While this policy primarily focuses on work-related use, it's important to remember:

  • Avoid during work hours/on company devices: Employees should avoid using AI Tools for personal purposes during working hours or on company devices.
  • Limit to non-sensitive topics: Any usage of AI Tools for personal purposes should be limited to non-sensitive topics and must not involve the disclosure of company-related information.
