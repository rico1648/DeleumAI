20. Roles and Responsibilities

Everyone has a part to play in ensuring responsible AI usage:

  • ICT Department: Responsible for overseeing the implementation, enforcement, and ongoing management of this policy.
  • Business and Department Heads: Responsible for ensuring that employees within their respective departments are aware of and comply with this policy.
  • Employees (YOU!): Responsible for adhering to this policy and its guidelines, participating in relevant training, and reporting any policy violations or security incidents.

Ethical Considerations for Employees:

  • Your AI is a Reflection of YOU. Remember, the onus is always on the user, not the tool. How you use AI reflects your professionalism and ethical judgment.
  • Your Data, Your Responsibility: Don't feed sensitive or private data into AI tools. You are responsible for ensuring that confidential company information, personal data, or intellectual property is never exposed through AI usage.
  • Your Integrity, Your Verification: Always verify the output generated by AI – it's a tool, not a truth engine. Do not blindly trust AI-generated content; review it carefully for accuracy, completeness, and appropriateness before use or sharing.
  • Your Compliance, Your Accountability: Comply with all regulatory and intellectual property (IP) standards. Ensure that your use of AI tools respects copyright laws and Deleum's intellectual property policies.
  • Your Purpose, Your Augmentation: Use AI responsibly – augment people, not replace them. AI tools are here to enhance your capabilities and improve efficiency, not to diminish human judgment or replace critical thinking.
