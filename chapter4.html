3. How Can I Use AI Tools for Work?

You are encouraged to use AI tools for work-related purposes! However, it's crucial that you always follow this policy and any other relevant company guidelines and protocols. When generating a report using AI tools, please disclose this to your colleagues. It's good practice to also save the prompts used. This allows others to review the process for potential bias or inaccuracies, and it helps the team learn and improve together through shared understanding and verification.

Examples of Authorized Usage:

  • Drafting emails or reports: AI can help you get started with a first draft, saving you time and effort.
  • Summarizing long documents: Need to quickly grasp the key points of a lengthy report? AI can help.
  • Brainstorming ideas: Use AI as a sounding board to generate new ideas for projects or presentations.
  • Data analysis (non-sensitive): AI can assist in identifying patterns or trends in data that does not contain sensitive information.
  • Research: Quickly find information on general topics to support your work.
