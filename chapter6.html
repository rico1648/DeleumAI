5. Controlling Your Data with AI Tools

To ensure that the content you share with AI tools isn't used by them to further train their language models, there are two important steps:

  • Disable data usage for model training: You must enable the feature within the AI tool that restricts data usage for model training. Most reputable AI tools have this option in their settings or privacy controls.
  • Delete your search history: After you've finished using an AI tool, remember to delete your search history within that tool. This helps to minimize any lingering data.
