SWOT Analysis of Deleum's AI Policy

A strategic overview of our AI governance framework, highlighting its internal strengths and weaknesses, alongside external opportunities and threats.

Strengths

  • Clear security guidelines
  • Mandatory employee training
  • Strong incident response plan
  • Emphasis on ethical use

Weaknesses

  • Potential to limit productivity
  • Relies on manual compliance
  • Heavy dependency on ICT
  • Vague on new tool approval

Opportunities

  • Boost innovation & efficiency
  • Strengthen data governance
  • Gain competitive advantage
  • Develop AI-literate workforce

Threats

  • Accidental data disclosure
  • Rapidly evolving AI landscape
  • Risk of non-compliance
  • Sophisticated cyber threats
